Game On is a all in one HTML/CSS theme with ability to change theme elements color, 2 layouts & easy to install. The theme also comes with a Full-width page, Left Sidebar Page and a Contact page.Game On is suitable for any business or personal website. The HTML markup for your tabs and content can be arranged however you want. At the minimum, you need a container, a collection of links for your tabs (an unordered list by default), and matching divs for your tabbed content.
